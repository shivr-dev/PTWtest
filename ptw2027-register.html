Step 2 — Select Exam Session
Choose your preferred test date and format. Seats are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis.

⚠️ Rescheduling Policy

Reschedule requests must be submitted at least 7 days before the exam date. A rescheduling fee of USD 25 applies. Maximum 2 reschedules per registration. Refunds (80% of fee) accepted up to 14 days before exam.

Step 3 — Upload Documents
All documents must be uploaded in JPEG, PNG, or PDF format. Files must be clear, unobstructed, and within the size limits.

📸 Applicant Photo Requirements

  • Recent photo taken within the last 6 months
  • Plain white or light grey background — no patterns, scenery, or other people
  • Face must be fully visible, looking straight at the camera
  • No sunglasses, hats, or head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Photo dimensions: 600×800 px minimum, aspect ratio 3:4
  • File size: 200 KB – 5 MB, JPEG or PNG only
  • Facial expression: neutral or slight smile, eyes open and clearly visible
  • No filters, heavy retouching, or digital alterations

🔒 Document Security

All documents are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and transmitted via TLS 1.3. Your ID is only used for identity verification and will not be shared with third parties. Documents are deleted from our servers 24 months after your exam date.

Step 4 — Review & Confirm
Please review all information carefully before submitting. Changes after submission require a formal amendment request.
📋 What Happens After Submission
  • Document review: 1–3 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation once approved.
  • If documents are rejected, you'll be notified with reasons and allowed to resubmit within 7 days.
  • Your seat is held during review but only confirmed upon document approval.
  • Score reports will be available in your portal 4 business days after your exam date.
